About the Paramore request page

This website was created by a Paramore fan (@TheParamoreBand / paramore.net.br). My name is Victor Brito and the purpose of this page is to make it easier for other fans to request Paramore songs on the radio stations around the world. All requests to stations from the US and Canada made using this page will actually be sent directly to Mediabase.

This website is not affiliated with Paramore or Fueled By Ramen.

Get in touch

Something's wrong? Want to include your country's radio stations in this website? Need help with Paramore related stuff or anything else you think I can help? Send an email to victor.brito@paramore.net.br and I'll reply as soon as I see your message!

Frequently Asked Questions

• What do I need to do?

Choose the radio station you want to send the request to, enter the captcha code (forced by Mediabase to avoid fake requests) and click send. As simple as that, since you'll have filled out the form in the settings page before making the first request.

• Why is my personal information needed?

Mediabase asks you to provide some information before requesting songs, but your name is actually the only required information. Still, I insist you fill out the whole form, I've read they may use the information (like zip code) to give priority to specific requests that coming from near a station.

• Where is my information stored?

As I said, all the requests are sent directly to Mediabase and your personal info will never go through my server; it's being stored in your own web browser, so you don't need to type it everytime you're making a request. It's faster and easier this way! ;)

• How many requests can I make?

Mediabase tracks your IP so you can only make 1 request per day to each radio station.
